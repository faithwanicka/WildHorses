Horses

Horses are amazing and beautiful creatures. Wild horese are scared of all animals (except thier own kind) because they are prey. Human tamed horses are gentle and caring but if the horse was treated badly by humans in the past (such as being beaten) or if a mare has a foal, or a colt, or is pregnant then the horse might lash out at a human.

Horses are swift as eagles and their fur is softer than a puppy's (not a wired haired haired one though). They are as smart an ape and can be as fierce as a lion when protecting themselves.
