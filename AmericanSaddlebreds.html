American Saddlebreds

American Saddlebreds, a breed of horse, is not a draft horse. They have finely chiseled headswith small, alert ears and large eyes; long arched neck; and are wide-chested with a short, strong back and poweful hind-quarters. American Saddlebreds can be born in colors. They are best suited for showing, trail riding, and pleasure riding. American Saddlebreds originate from the Eastern United States, especially Kentucky. The American Saddlebred also have special qualities such as elegance and beauty and having two extra gaits ( the slow gait and the rack ). These beautiful horses, with thier graceful bearing, are spirited yet gentle and easily trainable. They carry themselves elegantly and are a pleasure to ride and watch at all times.
