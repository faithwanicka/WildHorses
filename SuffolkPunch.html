Suffolk Punchs

Suffolk Punchs, a breed of horse, are draft horses. They have a large semetrical body; inteligent head and poweful arched neck; upright shoulders; deep heart girth and flank; short back, smooth quaters, level croup, and high set tail; close-set portionally short legs. Suffolk Punchs can be born in only chestnut and can sometimes have a small star or snip; occasionally white on their fetlocks. They are best suited for farming and logging. They originate from Suffolk, England. Suffolk Punchs have special qualities such as being extemely rare ( in England there are fewer than 100 of them ) and their world population is only 3,500.
